Car Ownership by Region Visualization

Car Ownership by Region

Victoria Leads in Car Ownership: Victoria stands out with over 720 cars per 1000 households, making it the region with the highest car ownership rate among all listed regions.

Tasmania and the Northern Territory Lag Behind: These two regions register the lowest car ownership rates with 580 and 560 cars per 1000 households, respectively.

Most Regions Show Consistent Ownership Patterns: The majority of the regions have car ownership rates ranging between 600 and 700, indicating a certain level of uniformity in car ownership across Australia.
